​1. Executive Summary
​The Axial Hybrids: Side-by-Side Platform is an open-source initiative designed to revolutionize the Electric Vehicle (EV) conversion industry. It provides a modular, "DIY-friendly" framework to transform existing Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) vehicles into high-performance hybrids. By leveraging local mechanical expertise and standardized components, we aim to accelerate the global transition to sustainable energy.
​2. Technical Architecture
​Core Hardware (QAP): Utilizes the Quantum-Axial Platform motor technology—an axial flux motor with a superior Torque-to-Weight ratio.
​Mechanical Interface: The QAP Universal Bellhousing Adaptor (Patent-Pending) allows the motor to mate directly with original manual or automatic transmissions (e.g., Toyota Revo/Vigo, Isuzu D-Max) without chassis modification.
​Software Intelligence: A Dual-Mode Controller utilizing proprietary Seamless Sync and Torque Matching algorithms to ensure smooth power delivery through original gearsets.
​Energy Infrastructure: A Modular Power Tray designed for the engine bay, housing battery packs and an optional Range Extender (REX) diesel generator for Series Hybrid functionality.
​3. Implementation & Support
​This platform empowers local mechanics. The modular design ensures that while the electric powertrain is sophisticated, the maintenance of the generator and basic mechanical interfaces can be handled by standard automotive workshops, drastically reducing the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO).
